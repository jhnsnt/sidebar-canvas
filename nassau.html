What is it like
in NASSAU?

Nassau is half resort paradise, half island street party — Atlantis, Cable Beach, and downtown Bay Street never stop.

Cable Beach and Paradise Island host mega-resort day clubs while downtown Bay Street and Arawak Cay turn into full-on street parties with live bands, conch cocktails, and that unmistakable cruise-ship crowd energy. Gather your crew and meet us in Nassau — we're ready, are you?

Enhance your Trip

Nassau has something for everyone with dance clubs, bars, parties, festivals, shows, restaurants, DJs, live bands, and more to choose from. During the day, the booze cruise is the most popular event of every spring break!

Party
Package

What is a Party Package? It's your entrance to the best concerts and nightlife that Nassau has to offer! Nassau has something for everyone, and this is especially true after dark. With numerous nightclubs to choose from, you can't go wrong. It's the ultimate nightlife for your ultimate Spring Break.

Bond Nightclub

Located inside Baha Mar — two floors, LED everything, and international DJ residencies every week.

Aura Nightclub

Atlantis Paradise Island's sky-high club — panoramic ocean views, bottle service, and the island's flashiest crowd.

Aquapazza Beach Club

Cable Beach's biggest day club — live DJs on the sand, infinity pool, and bottle service cabanas.

Fluid Nightclub

Downtown Nassau's late-night favorite — Caribbean beats, packed dance floor, and strong rum punch.

Señor Frog's Nassau

Cruise-ship crowd staple — foam parties, conga lines, and the trademark chaos the chain is known for.

Junkanoo Beach

Free public beach that turns into a street party every night — live bands, food trucks, and bonfires till sunrise.

Booze Cruise
& Other
Excursions

The Nassau booze cruise is a half-day catamaran from Paradise Island — open bar all the way, a snorkel stop at Athol Island, and Pig Beach if you add on the Exuma excursion.

Other Excursions & Day Tours

Need a break from the day parties, and want to explore other activities in Nassau? There are a wide range of excursions available so you can find exactly what you're looking for.

Swim with the pigs in Exuma, ride the Aquaventure slides at Atlantis, snorkel a sunken James Bond wreck, or take a day sail to the Blue Lagoon — Nassau has excursions for every type of traveler.
