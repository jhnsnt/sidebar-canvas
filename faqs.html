Frequently
Asked Questions.

"Everything you've ever wondered about booking spring break with us — answered."
How do I book a Go Blue Tours trip?

Pick a destination, get a quote, and your Account Executive walks you through everything. We handle the booking, sign waivers digitally in your portal, and ship your trip ID before you fly.

When should I book?

As early as possible. Top hotels for peak weeks (Mar 7–20) sell out by November. Booking before October typically saves 15–25% per person.

Can I book just for myself?

Yes. Solo travelers can join an existing group, room with same-gender travelers, or request a private room (additional supplement applies).

How much is the deposit?

$99 per person to lock in your spot. The rest can be paid in interest-free monthly installments through our financing partner — pay off the trip before you fly.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, ACH bank transfer, and the Go Blue installment plan.

Are there hidden fees?

No. Your quote includes hotel, transfers, party passes, taxes, and Go Blue concierge support. The only out-of-pocket items are airfare (unless added) and personal spending.

Do I need a passport?

Yes for any international destination (Cancún, Cabo, Punta Cana, Nassau, Freeport). Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months past your return date. Domestic Panama City Beach trips only need a valid US ID.

What if my passport is delayed?

We have a partnership with an expedited passport service that can deliver in as little as 5 business days. Talk to your Account Executive ASAP if you're in this situation.

Do I need a visa?

US citizens do not need a visa for Mexico, Dominican Republic, or the Bahamas for short tourist stays. Other passport holders should check requirements with their embassy.

What's included in my package?

Hotel stay (typically 5 or 7 nights), all-inclusive food and drinks (most resorts), airport transfers, party pass to our partner clubs, on-site Go Blue host, and 24/7 trip support.

Will I have a Go Blue staff member at my destination?

Always. Spring Break Hosts greet you at the airport, run the welcome party, and stay on-site at your hotel for the entire week.

What's the drinking age?

18 in Mexico, Dominican Republic, and the Bahamas. 21 in the United States. Always carry your passport or ID.

What if I need to cancel?

Refunds depend on how close you are to departure. With Travel Protection, you may be reimbursed up to 100% for covered reasons (or 75% for any reason on the Premium plan). Without Travel Protection, the deposit is non-refundable, and the cancellation schedule applies.

Can I transfer my booking to a friend?

Yes — up to 14 days before departure, for a small admin fee. Your AE handles the swap.

Can I change my dates or destination?

Date and destination changes are free up to 60 days before departure, subject to availability. After that, a change fee may apply.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes. Group rates kick in at 8 travelers and improve at 16, 32, and 50+. Group organizers also unlock free or discounted spots — see our Campus Manager program.

Do roommates have to book at the same time?

No, but they should book within the same week to lock the rate. Your AE keeps the room held while everyone confirms.

How big can my group get?

No limit — we've moved entire chapters of 200+. Larger groups get a dedicated planner, private welcome party, and a free room block strategy session.
